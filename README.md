# Wes Mantooth Digital Forensic Investigation
Digital forensic investigation of the Wes Mantooth laptop case

## Overview

This project is a digital forensic investigation of a laptop belonging to Wes Mantooth.  
The goal of the investigation was to analyze the device and identify digital evidence that could connect the suspect to criminal activities.

---

## Tools Used

- FTK Imager
- Forensic Explorer

---

## Disk Imaging

The first step of the investigation was creating a forensic image of the hard drive using **FTK Imager**.  
This ensures the original evidence is preserved and the analysis is performed on a copy of the data. 

**Screenshot:**

![FTK Imaging](https://i.imgur.com/6dKd7yw.png)

## Registry Analysis

The Windows Registry was examined to identify system information and user accounts stored on the device.

The system contained several user accounts including:

- Administrator  
- Guest  
- Wes Mantooth  
- Dracula  
- Laurent

## Documents Discovered

The forensic investigation uncovered multiple documents related to criminal activities including:

- drug transaction records
- suspicious spreadsheets
- personal notes

These files suggested involvement in illegal operations.

## Emails

Several emails were recovered that indicated communication between Wes Mantooth and John Washer about illegal activities.

These communications suggest the two individuals were involved in criminal operations together.

**Screenshot:**

![Emails](https://i.imgur.com/lLIJhyr.png)

## Encryption Evidence

The investigation also discovered encrypted files and the presence of encryption software (VeraCrypt).  
This suggests that the suspect attempted to hide sensitive information.
